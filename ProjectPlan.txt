Objects:
t = growing tree
    - Grows in 8+(0-4) years
T = grown tree
    - Gives one point
g = grass
    - If 6+/8 surrounding tiles are green for 1+(0-4) years will turn into 't'
d = dirt
    - If 1+/8 surrounding tiles are green for 1+(0-4) years will turn into (g)
s = stone
    - Only non-natural tile
